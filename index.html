Golf Course Services

PRO SHOP

FOR ALL YOUR NEEDS

Our pro shop offers it all. We carry all the latest in golf clubs, golf balls, golf bags, golf gloves, golf clothing, golf shoes and golf accessories. Our employees are highly trained and can offer advice about any product.

GOLF CARTS

ALL DAY RENTALS

We carry the finest golf carts in Ontario. Our carts are quiet, virtually vibration free, and have the lowest emissions around. We also offer solar powered golf carts as well. All of our carts offer exceptional mileage.

DRINK SERVICE

SERVED ON EACH HOLE

Need a refreshing drink but don't feel like walking to the clubhouse. Let us serve you directly on a hole. We offer a wide variety of beers, coolers and mixed drinks. A large assortment of snacks are also available.

Our clubhouse was built in 1920 and refurbished in 2012. Our staff of chefs can cook anything you desire. We also have over 20 craft beers on tap.
